Co-op Crafting Game — Server-Authoritative Systems & Clear UX

Inventory, recipes, collectables, and celebration effects—built for fairness, speed, and designer friendliness.

I built the core systems for a cooperative crafting game on Roblox, focusing on smooth gameplay and server-side security. The inventory runs on the server, sends clean snapshots to clients, and mirrors items into in-game tools so players always know what they own. A recipe tracker validates submissions and updates a large shared “Wanting Screen” with clear text and color-coded progress.

Collectables are easy to expand: tag an object and it becomes pickable with a prompt, then it automatically respawns around the map. A celebration flow rewards players with currency, plays effects, and resets the screen when a recipe is complete. Together, these choices keep the game fair, responsive, and easy to build on.
